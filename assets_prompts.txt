==============================================================================
AQUARIUM ASSET GENERATION PROMPTS  (Nano Banana Pro)
==============================================================================

EDITING CONTRACT (why the prompts are shaped this way)
------------------------------------------------------
Two pipelines:

BACKGROUNDS (coral_reef, sunken_ship, deep_ocean) -> full-color OPAQUE scenes.
- Real ocean water at the top, real seabed floor at the bottom -> NO magenta, NO
  chroma key, NO transparency; the scene fills the frame edge-to-edge.
- A smooth vertical water gradient + a graded floor ARE allowed here (the only
  place gradients are permitted) -> matches the reference look, exports to WebP.
- No sparkles / glints / god-rays / floating particles on the open water -> stays
  clean and graphic.

PROP SPRITES (the PROP SPRITES section) -> still on solid #FF00FF magenta for
one-pass chroma key to transparency, so they overlay on the backgrounds at runtime.
- No magenta / neon-pink inside the prop art -> the key never bites the artwork.
- Flat solid colors, crisp edges, no gradients/noise -> compresses to a tiny
  transparent WebP, with the form preserved.

Each background's final prompt = its own [STYLE] + [DEPTH & PHYSICS] + [BACKGROUND]
+ [CONSTRAINTS] + [SUBJECT] (self-contained per theme). Each prop's final prompt =
the shared PROP SPRITES blocks + ONE [SUBJECT] line.


==============================================================================
THEME BACKGROUNDS  (full-color opaque scenes - 16:9, >= 2048x1152)
==============================================================================
Each theme below is self-contained: feed its [STYLE] + [DEPTH & PHYSICS] +
[BACKGROUND] + [CONSTRAINTS] + [SUBJECT] together as one prompt.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THEME: coral_reef
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[STYLE]
Flat 2D vector illustration in a clean, friendly cartoon aquarium-game style. Bold
SOLID flat color fills with crisp, smooth vector edges and clear, consistent dark
line-art outlines (dark brown or navy, NOT pure black). Limited palette; at most
simple 2-3 step cel-shading on the scenery - NO airbrushing, texture, grain, noise,
or dithering on the scenery. Strict orthographic SIDE view at eye level (2D
side-scroller look) - NOT top-down, NOT 3D, no camera tilt. Clean shapes with a
storybook-game feel.

[DEPTH & PHYSICS]
Convey depth with 2-3 overlapping layers receding backward: nearer structures
larger, more saturated and more detailed; farther structures rendered as DISTINCT
flat, cooler, desaturated silhouettes standing in the water with visible contrast
(underwater atmospheric perspective) - distinct flat tonal layers, NOT a blurry
haze. The ONLY gradients allowed are the water column and the seabed floor; the
scenery itself stays flat cel-shaded. Light from above. Keep contact with the sand
natural; do not paint a separate hard cast shadow onto the open water.

[BACKGROUND]
A full, opaque underwater scene - NO magenta, NO transparency, NO checkerboard. The
water fills the upper ~75% of the frame as a smooth vertical gradient: icy pale cyan
(#D7F0FC) at the very top, easing through soft sky-cyan to a medium ocean cyan-blue
(#78C3E0) lower down. A thin, slightly brighter teal waterline strip marks where the
water meets the seabed. The lower ~25% is a warm pale sandy floor (#E8DDBF), gently
graded, spanning the full width. Scenery rises from this sand; open water and sand
fill all the gaps.

[CONSTRAINTS]
Absolutely NO sparkles, stars, glints, twinkles, light specks, particles, bubbles,
bokeh, god-rays, light shafts, or lens flare anywhere. NO fish, animals, divers, or
creatures. NO text, numbers, watermark, logo, UI, frame, or border. NO photographic
realism, grain, or noise. Gradients are allowed ONLY in the water column and the
sandy floor - keep all scenery flat cel-shaded.

[SUBJECT] - coral_reef   (16:9)
A vibrant coral reef as three distinct clustered outcrops rising from the sandy
floor with open cyan water between them. Each combines branching staghorn corals,
rounded brain corals, fan corals, and tube sponges in flat reds, coral-pink, orange,
violet and teal-green, with a few green anemones and short seagrass tufts on small
rocky mounds. Healthy, lively, tropical, unmistakably a coral reef. Nearer corals
larger and brighter; the far depth layer is a set of distinct cooler lavender/purple
coral silhouettes standing in the water behind them.

glowing cyan and teal dots and tips, never the whole organism lit. Deep, calm and
lonely, gently lit.


==============================================================================
PROP SPRITES
==============================================================================

[STYLE]
Flat 2D vector game-sprite art, clean friendly cartoon aquarium style. Bold SOLID
flat color fills, crisp smooth vector edges, limited palette, at most simple 2-3
step cel-shading - NO gradients, airbrushing, texture, noise, or dithering. No
hard black outlines (thin soft darker-tone outline at most). Orthographic
side/front view at eye level. Clean shapes that scale down crisply and trace to
vector cleanly.

[DEPTH & PHYSICS]
Show the object's volume with flat cel-shaded tone steps and clear light-from-
above. A single, solid, well-rounded form. Do NOT add any cast or contact shadow
on the background - the object sits on pure magenta (the app adds its own shadow
at runtime).

[BACKGROUND]
Render on a SINGLE SOLID FLAT MAGENTA background, hex #FF00FF, filling the whole
frame uniformly. One object, centered, filling ~80% of the frame, with a clean
even margin of pure magenta all around. No checkerboard, no transparency, no
gradient, no second object, no ground line - just the object on flat magenta.

[CONSTRAINTS]
NO sparkles, stars, glints, twinkles, specks, particles, bubbles, bokeh, or lens
flare. NO magenta/neon-pink/hot-pink in the artwork (use coral-pink/rose/salmon/
violet). NO cast or drop shadow on the magenta. NO text, watermark, frame,
border, or extra objects. NO photographic realism, gradients, noise, grain, or
texture.

==============================================================================
OUTPUT SETTINGS
==============================================================================
- Backgrounds: full-color OPAQUE scene, NO magenta / NO chroma key. 16:9,
               >= 2048x1152. One theme per image; export to WebP.
- Prop sprites: one subject per image on a solid #FF00FF magenta background
                (chroma-keyed to transparency). 1024x1024, TALL ones 1024x1536.
- Filenames:   backgrounds use background.webp under the theme folder; props use
               the name shown (coral_red.webp, sunken_ship.webp, ...).
- Consistency: generate each background from its own self-contained blocks, and
               reuse the shared PROP SPRITES blocks for every prop. Once you have
               one you like, attach it as a style reference to keep the rest
               consistent.
